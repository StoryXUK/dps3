Services

For each project we establish relationships with partners

We turn ideas into works of art.

At DPS Stonework, we offer a comprehensive range of stone masonry services, tailored to meet the unique needs of both domestic and commercial clients across Cornwall and Devon. Our services include:

Sea Walls

Durable flood defence and sea walls, expertly crafted to protect properties from coastal erosion and flooding with long-lasting stone solutions.

Retaining Walls

Strong, aesthetically pleasing retaining walls that support landscape structures while enhancing your property with expertly engineered stonework.

Cavity Face Work

Precision cavity face work providing superior insulation, weather resistance, and a flawless finish to your building’s exterior stone walls.

Lime Mortar and Lime Plastering

We use lime mortar and plaster in both restoration and new build projects, appreciating its flexibility, breathability, and durability.

Garden Walls

Beautifully crafted garden walls, defining outdoor spaces with expert stonework that adds charm, privacy, and structure to any landscape.

Stone Features

We create custom stone features that are as unique as our clients. From garden sculptures and water features to intricate stone carvings, we bring your ideas to life with skill and creativity.

1. Consultation & Design

Begin with an in-depth consultation to understand your vision and requirements. DPS experts will then create a tailored design plan that aligns with your goals and budget.

2. Material Selection

Choose from a range of high-quality stones and materials. DPS will guide you in selecting the best options to ensure durability, aesthetics, and project success.

3. Expert Craftsmanship

Skilled masons execute the project with precision and care, ensuring each detail meets the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.

4. Project Completion

Upon completion, DPS conducts a thorough inspection to ensure the work meets all expectations. The project is only finalized when you are fully satisfied.
