About DPS Stonework

With years of experience in the stone masonry industry, DPS Stonework has earned a reputation for excellence throughout Cornwall and Devon. Our team is passionate about preserving the heritage and charm of the region's architecture, while also pushing the boundaries of modern stonework design. We combine traditional techniques with contemporary practices to ensure that each project is both authentic and enduring.

From the careful restoration of historical landmarks to the creation of bespoke features in new builds, our commitment to quality is evident in every detail. We pride ourselves on delivering personalised service, working closely with our clients to understand their needs and exceed their expectations.

Locations Served

We cover all of Cornwall and Devon, including Penzance, St Ives, Coverack, Falmouth, Hayle, Truro, Newquay, St Austell, Wadebridge, Bodmin, Liskeard, Duloe, and Looe. In Devon, our services extend to Plymouth, Totnes, Exeter, Torquay, Dartmoor, Plympton, Tavistock, and everywhere in between.
